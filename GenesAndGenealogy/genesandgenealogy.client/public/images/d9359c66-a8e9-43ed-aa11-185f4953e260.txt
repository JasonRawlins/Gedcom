Dylan Davis, son of Owen Davis (1930-1990) and Gwen Jones (1900-1960), was born in Merthyr Tydfil, Glamorgan, Wales on 7 July 1930. He studied Welsh history at the Aberystwyth University from 1948-1952. He received a Bachelor's degree with honours. He married Eithne Lynch in Scotland in 1953. However, they were divorced one year later. He then married Fiona Douglas in Scotland in 1955. Davis spent much of his career in publishing. He had one daughter, Sarah Davis, in 1960. In 1980, he was elected councillor for a city advisory board. He died of pneumonia on 7 July 1990 at the local hospital in Cardiff, Wales.
